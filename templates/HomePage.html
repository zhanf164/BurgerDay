What is it?

New York burger day is an adventure dreamed up by a few friends late one night at a rooftop bar in Washington DC. The Main goal: eat three of New York's best burger offerings in a single day. The original idea was to train into Manhattan from Washington DC in the morning, eat the three burgers before midnight, with the idea of training back to DC that same evening. However, one group member, who shall remain nameless, was adamant that one night must be spent in New York.

Locations

New York Burger Day features three of the premiere burgers in New York. The route can be completed in any order, but must include the following restaurants: Emily (either location), Peter Lugers, and P.J Clark's.
